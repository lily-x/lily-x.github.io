Ph.D. student at USC

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at the University of Southern California, advised by Prof. Milind Tambe and Prof. Bistra Dilkina. I seek to apply research in computer science — specifically in machine learning, game theory, and discrete optimization — to issues in sustainability. I am part of the USC Center for AI in Society, whose mission is to advance artificial intelligence for social good.

Before joining USC, I received my bachelor's degree in computer science with a minor in Spanish from Dartmouth College in 2018.
